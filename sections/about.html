The Shape of the Water

A warm welcome and a message from Vanduo Foundation Open Source Board founding members

We invite you to explore our philosophy, and we genuinely appreciate the time you are taking to read our story.

The fact that you're here, spending a few of your minutes with us, means more than any star count or download metric ever could. So before anything else: thank you.

First, what Vanduo Framework is not

Let's clear the air.

Vanduo is not a competitor to Nuxt, Vue, or React. We are not here to replace your favorite tools, start a framework war, or convince you to rewrite your perfectly working application. Those projects are remarkable feats of engineering, maintained by brilliant people, and they solve real problems at enormous scale. We respect them deeply.

Vanduo does not operate on a Virtual DOM. There is no reconciliation algorithm running behind the scenes, no diffing tree, no synthetic event system. And that's by design — not by limitation.

So what is Vanduo?

Vanduo is the best friend of Dynamic Web, SPA, Lazy Loading, and Static Web alike.

Think of it this way: not every page on the internet needs a reactive state machine. Not every portfolio, documentation site, or landing page needs to ship a JavaScript runtime to the browser before a single word of text appears on screen. But those pages do deserve to appear beautiful, feel responsive, and load instantly.

That's where Vanduo lives.

We built Vanduo for the projects that come before and around the complex ones — the spaces where HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of vanilla JavaScript are not just enough, but are the right tool for the job. Vanduo gives those projects a design system that would make any SPA jealous, without asking you to install a single dependency.

Why we built this

We started Vanduo because we noticed something: the web had become heavy. Not heavy in a bad-faith way — heavy because, somewhere along the line, the simplest possible website began requiring a node_modules folder, a bundler config, and a PhD in toolchain orchestration.

We asked ourselves: what if we went the other way?

What if we stripped away everything that doesn't serve the developer or the end user? What if we treated constraints — zero dependencies, no build step, no npm install — not as limitations, but as a creative compass?

The answer was water. Essential, transparent, adaptable. Vanduo.

Our hopes, dreams, and ambitions

We hope to keep Vanduo lean. Every kilobyte should earn its place. If something doesn't serve you or your users, we want the courage to leave it out.

We dream of keeping Vanduo honest. No magic, no black boxes. You can read every line we ship — and we hope you will. Understanding your tools shouldn't require a decoder ring.

We aspire to keep Vanduo open — not open as a marketing strategy, but open as a principle. This is not a product with a free tier. This is a gift to the community, maintained with care, documented with pride, and released under a license that means what it says.

And above all, our deepest ambition is to keep Vanduo for you. Not for hype cycles, not for benchmarks, not for conference talks — for the developer sitting at a desk at 2 AM who just wants their site to look great without fighting a framework.

We're not there yet on all of these. But having the right ambitions matters, and these are ours.

For every kind of builder

The way people build for the web is changing — fast.

There are vibe coders spinning up entire projects in an afternoon with AI at the wheel. There are weekend builders turning side ideas into real products between Saturday coffee and Sunday dinner. There are solo devs wearing every hat, small teams punching above their weight, and hustlers who need their landing page live yesterday.

We see all of you. And we built Vanduo with all of you in mind.

Our ambition is to make Vanduo AI-friendly while being developer-friendly first — and we mean every kind of developer:

  • The full AI vibe coder who wants to describe a page and watch it materialize.
  • The hybrid developer who pairs with AI but keeps their hands on the wheel.
  • The no-AI purist who wants to understand every single line and wouldn't have it any other way.

Vanduo works for all three because there is nothing hidden. No abstraction layer to confuse a language model. No magic syntax that only makes sense if you've read 400 pages of documentation. Just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the languages that both humans and AI already speak fluently.

That's also why we've already started including llms.txt files in our repository — structured, AI-readable summaries of the entire framework. It's a small step, but it's a natural evolution of how we build the web. Because if the future of development is a conversation between human creativity and machine capability, we want Vanduo to be a language they both understand.

A small thing can matter

We don't need to be the biggest framework. We don't need to be on every trending list. If Vanduo helps even one developer ship a project they're proud of — a project that loads in a blink, looks polished on every device, and was fun to build — then everything we've poured into this was worth it.

Water doesn't compete with fire. It doesn't compete with earth. It just flows, adapts, and gives life wherever it goes.

That's the shape of the water. That's Vanduo.

We invite you to let your ideas flow just as freely. Join us, and let's build something lasting together.

Mission

To give every developer a framework that feels like writing plain HTML again -- simple enough to start in minutes, powerful enough to ship real products. No abstractions to learn, no toolchains to configure, just water-clear code.

Vision

A web where lightweight sites are the norm, not the exception. Where choosing simplicity is never a compromise on beauty, accessibility, or performance.

Constraints

Zero dependencies. No build step. No npm install. Every kilobyte earns its place. Vanduo ships as pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript -- if a feature can't justify its weight, it doesn't get in. These limits aren't restrictions; they're the reason the framework stays fast, portable, and honest.

Perspective

We picture a development landscape where reaching for a massive framework is a choice, not a reflex. Vanduo exists so that static sites, landing pages, and documentation can look polished without pulling in hundreds of packages.

Discipline

No transpilers, no bundlers, no runtime surprises. The entire framework fits in a single stylesheet and one ES module. Browser-native features first; polyfills never.

Essence

Strip away everything that doesn't serve the developer or the end user. What remains is Vanduo -- essential, like water.

What "Vanduo" Means

Vanduo (pronounced van-doo-oh) is derived from Lithuanian: vanduo means "water."

Just as water is essential for life, we believe a good CSS framework should be essential yet unobtrusive -- flowing naturally into your projects without forcing you to change your workflow. Water adapts to its container; Vanduo adapts to your design.
